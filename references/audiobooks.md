# Audiobook Reference (Updated March 2026)

## Audiobook Market Context
- Global audiobook market growing rapidly; audiobook usage up 36% in recent years
- Audible holds ~63% US market share; Spotify/INaudio growing fast (35% rise in listening hours Jan 2024–Jan 2025)
- AI narration now accepted on most major platforms — distribution options expanding rapidly

## Production Options

### Option A: Human Narration
**Best for**: Premium positioning, fiction, memoirs, where author voice matters

- **Self-narrate**: record in treated space, 6–8" from mic, avoid dairy/caffeine before
- **Hire narrator**: ACX marketplace, Fiverr, Upwork — budget $150–$400/finished hour
- **ACX royalty share**: narrator records for free in exchange for 50% royalties + exclusivity — avoids upfront cost but expensive long-term

### Option B: AI Narration — ElevenLabs Studio (recommended)
**Best for**: Non-fiction, cost-effective backlist conversion, speed, multi-language

**ElevenLabs Studio workflow:**
1. Get Creator Plan ($22/month) — required for Professional Voice Cloning + Commercial Licence
   - Do not use Starter — credit limit too low for a full book
2. Upload your EPUB, DOCX, or PDF to ElevenLabs Studio
3. Studio converts chapter by chapter automatically
4. Select or clone a voice (Professional Voice Cloning for consistency across long content)
5. Review per-chapter; adjust pronunciation and pacing using fine-tuning controls
6. Export as MP3 files per chapter

**Cost estimate for a 50,000-word book:**
- Creator Plan: $22/month
- Credits needed: ~250,000 (book + revisions)
- Total: approximately $60–$80
- vs. human narrator: $2,000–$5,000+

**Fixing typos/errors post-production**: fix text in source → click Regenerate → re-export. Instant, free.

**Voice cloning your own voice**: builds a reusable asset for all future books, podcast intros, marketing clips.

### Option C: KDP Virtual Voice (Amazon's free AI narration)
**Available**: US authors only, beta programme, invite-based access
**Cost**: Free
**Process**: In KDP dashboard → select eligible ebook → "Generate Virtual Voice Audiobook"
**Voices**: 80 options including American/British/Australian English, Spanish, French, Italian. Different voice per chapter possible.
**Pricing**: $3.99–$14.99 (you set the price)
**Royalty**: 40% for à la carte (ALC) sales
**Distribution**: Amazon/Audible only
**Limitation**: Amazon's AI — lower quality than ElevenLabs v3

## Distribution Platforms

### ACX (Audiobook Creation Exchange)
**URL**: acx.com | **Owned by**: Amazon/Audible
**Distributes to**: Amazon, Audible, Apple Books (Audible arrangement)
**Royalties**:
- Exclusive (7 years): 40% of list price
- Non-exclusive: 25% of list price
- Royalty share with narrator: 20% each (exclusive, 7 years)

**AI narration policy**:
- Cannot upload ElevenLabs files as "human narrated" — that's a TOS violation
- Use KDP Virtual Voice for Amazon AI audiobooks instead
- ACX is primarily for human-narrated content

**Exclusivity warning**: ACX exclusive means NO other platform (not Spotify, not Google, not Apple directly). Libraries spend almost 24% of their budgets on audiobooks — ACX exclusive means no library sales. Non-exclusive ACX is the better long-term choice.

### INaudio (formerly Findaway Voices by Spotify)
**Rebranded**: August 1, 2025 (was Findaway Voices)
**URL**: voices.inaudio.com (or via Spotify for Authors)
**Royalties**: 80% of net revenue
**Cost**: Free
**ALLi rating**: Excellent (Partner member)
**Distributes to**: Spotify, Audible, Apple Books, bibliotheca, 30+ other retailers and library systems including: Audiobooks Now, Chirp, Downpour, Everand, Google Play, Hoopla, Kobo, Libro.FM, Nextory, OverDrive, Storytel, and more

**AI narration**: ✅ Accepted — ElevenLabs files accepted since February 2025. Must check "AI/Synthesized Voice" checkbox at upload.

**Best for**: Wide audiobook distribution (equivalent to D2D for ebooks), non-exclusive, Spotify-connected audience

---

### PublishDrive (Audiobooks)
**URL**: publishdrive.com
**Fee**: Monthly subscription (starts ~$13.99/month billed yearly)
**Royalties**: You keep 100% net royalties — PublishDrive takes no cut
**Distributes to**: 50 distribution partners including Apple Audiobooks, Audible, Google Play, Kobo, OverDrive, Storytel, and more
**AI narration**: Accepted — must indicate "AI Narration" in metadata

---

### Google Play Books (Audiobooks)
**Direct auto-narration**: Available if your ebook is on Google Play — Google's own AI narration tool
**ElevenLabs files**: Accepted via INaudio/Findaway Voices distribution

## Recommended Audiobook Strategy

### Non-exclusive wide (best long-term):
1. Produce with ElevenLabs Studio (~$70 for a standard book)
2. Upload to **INaudio** (Spotify network — 30+ platforms including Spotify, Apple, Google, Audible)
3. Upload to **PublishDrive** (catches platforms INaudio misses, including some European markets)
4. Upload to **KDP Virtual Voice** (for Amazon-only AI version — use Amazon's own system to avoid TOS issues)

### Budget/quick option:
1. KDP Virtual Voice (free, Amazon only)
2. INaudio (free, wide distribution)

### Human narration (premium):
1. ACX non-exclusive (Amazon/Audible/Apple)
2. INaudio (everywhere else)

## Audiobook Technical Specs (ACX / INaudio requirements)

- **Format**: MP3 (192 kbps or higher)
- **Sample rate**: 44.1 kHz
- **Channels**: Mono or stereo
- **Noise floor**: -60 dBFS or lower
- **Peak levels**: -3 dBFS max
- **Room tone**: consistent throughout
- **File structure**: one file per chapter + retail audio sample (first 5 minutes)
- **Cover**: square JPG, minimum 2400 × 2400 px, 72 DPI min

## ACX Retail Audio Sample
The first 5 minutes of audio (including any intro/preface) become the free preview on Audible. Ensure this is your strongest, most engaging content.
